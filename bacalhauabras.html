Bacalhau à Brás

Ingredients

  • 500g (1 lb) of shredded and desalted codfish
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 200ml (1 cup) of heavy cream
  • 500g (1 lb) of potatoes, cut into thin matchsticks
  • 2 eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Black olives and chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat a generous amount of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced onions and cook until they are soft and translucent.
  4. Add the shredded codfish and cook for a few minutes until it is heated through.
  5. Add the matchstick potatoes and cook until they are golden brown and crispy.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream.
  7. Finish with the Eggs
    1. Egg and cream mixture over the codfish and potatoes in the skillet.
  8. Stir gently to combine and cook until the eggs are just set.
  9. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and crispy.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
  11. Garnish with black olives and chopped parsley before serving.
